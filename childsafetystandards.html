Supergram Child Safety Standards
Last updated: 12th November 2025
Supergram is strictly an 18+ social media app.
No minors are allowed to register or access our services.
Even though our platform is adult-only, we remain fully committed to preventing any
form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).
1. Zero-Tolerance Policy for CSAE
Supergram enforces an absolute zero-tolerance policy toward:
Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
Sexualized content involving minors
Grooming, enticement, or predatory behavior
Sharing, requesting, or distributing personal information of minors
Any violation will result in instant permanent account termination and may be
reported to law enforcement.
2. Age Requirements
Supergram is for adults aged 18 and above only.
Users attempting to misrepresent their age will be removed.
3. Proactive Detection & Reporting
We use:
Automated detection systems
Manual moderation
User reporting tools
CSAE violations are promptly reported to:
Local law enforcement
National cybercrime authorities
(as legally required)
4. Minor Protection Measures
Even though minors are not allowed, Supergram includes safeguards such as:
Monitoring for accounts attempting to impersonate minors
Detection of minor-related keywords and content
Abuse reporting tools on all posts, reels, proles, and messages
5. User Reporting Tools
Users can report harmful or unsafe content through:
Report on posts and messages
Report user on proles
Reports are reviewed 24/7 by our safety team.
6. Safety Education & Support
We provide resources and reminders to help users follow safety practices and avoid
harmful interactions.
7. Contact Information
To report any CSAE-related violation, contact us immediately:
arjun@supergram.in
We prioritize all child-safety investigations and respond as quickly as possible.
