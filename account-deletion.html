Supergram – Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your Supergram account and all associated personal data directly from within the app. We provide a simple and transparent process that allows you to manage and erase your data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account (Inside the App)

Follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Supergram app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Account
  3. Select “Deactivate” or “Delete My Account”
  4. Confirm your deletion request

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 days, as per our data retention policy.

Important Notes

  • You will lose access to all your account data after deletion.
  • Some system logs may be retained for security, anti-abuse, and legal reasons.
  • You can reinstall the app and create a new account anytime.
